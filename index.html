Welcome to Yoink

Adaptive QM/MM methods repartition molecules into the QM and MM regions on the fly based on a partitioning criterion. A density based partitioning scheme offers a unique way of basing the QM and MM assignments on the presence/absence of (non-) covalent interactions. Yoink can also perform the alternative distance or number based partitionings.
